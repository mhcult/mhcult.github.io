About Us

Daisy Chain was founded in 2005 by a small group of frisbee loving individuals. Currently, we represent Mount Holyoke College's competive womxn's ultimate team. The team has progressed immensely since the sport was introduced to MHC. We have qualified for the D-III College Championships every year since 2016. We have an amazing and supportive team this year, and we look forward to throwing discs, wobbling, eating pasta, and qualifiying for Nationals again!
